About this project

This site is a home for simple, transparent economics & tax tools. The goal is to help people explore ‘what‑if’ questions without jargon.

How the budget simulator works: It uses a stylized, educational model with nominal GDP growth, average interest on federal debt, and a chosen primary balance (surplus/deficit) as a percent of GDP. It’s not a forecast — it’s a learning tool.
